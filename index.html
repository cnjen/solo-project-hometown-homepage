Visit Denmark

Enjoy gorgeous nature and windy coastlines in this small country of the North

Top three activities to do in Denmark

Mols Bjerge

Go hiking in the beautiful nature of Mols Bjerge and meet the Exmoor ponies and Galloway cattle reintroduced to Denmark through a rewilding project.

The West Coast

The West Coast of Jutland is perfect for anyone who loves sand between their toes, fresh wind in their hair, and the sound of big waves arriving at shore.

Seal Safari

Grab the unique opportunity to go for a boat safari on Limfjorden and learn about Denmark's native seal species.
